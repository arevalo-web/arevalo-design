DECEMBER 2025 ALBUM ROLLOUT

THE PROBLEM

Lil Uzi Vert’s visual history is defined by psychedelic saturation. This rollout required a total departure from that legacy to establish a new, celestial era for the artist.

THE RESULT

A cinematic narrative following a cosmic entity through high-impact environments. This reimagined release positions the artist’s visual identity as a refined, ethereal progression rather than a repeat of previous eras.
